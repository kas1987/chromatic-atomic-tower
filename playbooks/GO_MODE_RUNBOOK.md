# GO Mode Runbook

`GO` means resolve the highest-priority approved unblocked BEAD and dispatch only that BEAD.

## Procedure

1. Read mission registry.
2. Identify approved active mission.
3. Identify approved unblocked BEAD.
4. Confirm allowed paths and stop conditions.
5. Select agent/model route.
6. Dispatch.
7. Capture evidence.
8. Validate.
9. Score confidence.
10. Queue next BEAD or close.

## Anti-patterns

- Do not search broadly for work.
- Do not invent architecture.
- Do not continue without evidence.
- Do not promote without gate results.
